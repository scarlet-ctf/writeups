The Legend of Girlie Pop

Established in 2024, Girlie Pop's Pizza Place was founded on a dream: to combine animatronic entertainment with an overload of pink aesthetics. We took over the old family diner location (you know the one, with the weird rumors) and gave it a complete makeover!
